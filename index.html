A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Srimad Bhuvanendra Educational Institutions (SBRS) was founded on 01-06-2001 to fulfill the educational needs of children in rural areas. Nestled in 14 acres of lavish greenery at the foothills of the Western Ghats, we provide a serene environment for holistic development.

Guided by the vision of Padmashree Dr. T.M.A. Pai, we blend academic rigour with discipline, culture, and values.

Principal's Message

"We strive to instill quality and discipline in every student, preparing them not just for exams, but for life."
