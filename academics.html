Teaching Methodology

The school has maintained the highest educational standards. We adopt practical measures to improve the quality of education:

  • Effective Tutorial System
  • Continuous Evaluation
  • Encouragement for independent study
  • Library reading and seminars

Examination & Evaluation

A continuous assessment of the students is made through regular home assignments, class tests, and terminal examinations. A Cumulative Record is maintained by every student which forms a dependable record of progress, conduct, and attendance.
